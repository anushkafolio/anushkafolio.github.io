About Me

A teacher who believes in every student's potential.

I'm an educator with a deep love for the humanities. With dual Master's degrees in English Literature and Political Science — and a Bachelor of Education from Kumaun University — I bring both breadth and depth to my teaching practice.

In the classroom, I focus on structured learning, meaningful assessment, and meeting every student where they are. Whether it's helping a student craft a strong evidence-based paragraph or using exit tickets to catch misconceptions early, I care about getting the details right.

Beyond teaching, I stay curious — exploring topics from the economics of artificial intelligence to global health governance through continued learning. I also enjoy debate, art, and calligraphy.

I'm currently teaching in India and preparing to bring my qualifications and experience to schools in the United Kingdom.
